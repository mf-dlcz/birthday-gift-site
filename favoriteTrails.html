Dawn Mine Trail

Dawn Mine, located near Altadena, CA, offers a picturesque 5.5-mile loop trail. Dogs are welcome, but they must be kept on a leash throughout the hike. The trail is known for its moderate challenge level, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced hikers. On average, it takes about 3 hours to complete the loop. I highly recommend this scenic route, and I hope you have the opportunity to experience it for yourself.
