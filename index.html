Top three activities to do in Los Angeles

Discover a picturesque hiking trail

Los Angeles offers a diverse range of options that traverse stunning landscapes and showcase the city's natural beauty.

Stroll through a stunning botanical garden

Explore the lush beauty of Los Angeles by visiting its enchanting botanical gardens, where a captivating array of plant life awaits your discovery.

Admire an extensive collection of works of art

Los Angeles offers a diverse array of world-class art museums, ranging from the iconic LACMA to the contemporary exhibits at The Broad, providing a rich and immersive artistic experience.
